About Aguilar Capital Markets

Aguilar Capital has an understanding of the unique challenges entrepreneurs face with respect to accessing affordable funding for capital expenditure, acquisitions and other tranformative transactions.

We have cultivated and continue to work on broadening relationships with banks, development financial institutions, emerging market focues funds and other financiers to ensure that we source the best funding solution on behalf of our clientele. We are keenly aware of the difficulty acccessing capital and the usurious terms offered by local financial institutions that is a common occurence in most African Markets. We recognize this as a structural problem that will take decades to resolve. Your business cannot wait for this to straighten out if you want to position your business for suitable growth.
